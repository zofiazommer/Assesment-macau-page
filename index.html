Macau is an autonomous region on the south coast of China. It has also earned the nickname of "Las Vegas of Asia"




When to go and how to get there

The best time to go to Macau is between October and December as it is a post-summer pre-winter preiod which has the perfect conditions for visiting.

The best way to reach Macau is through ferry companies that operate from Hong Kong to Macau at cheap prices.

Things to see and do

The best places to see are the following:

  • Senado Square
  • A-Ma Temple
  • Coloane
  • Monte Fort
  • Macau Grand Prix museum
  • Walls of St. Paul church
  • Macau Tower convention
  • City of Dreams
